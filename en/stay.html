1.1.1 University-Managed Student Apartments

There are mainly four student dormitories managed by Saarland University:

  • Studentenwohnheim E (Student Dormitory E)
  • Studentenwohnheim Waldhaus (Waldhaus Student Dormitory)
  • Studentenwohnheim Guckelsberg (Guckelsberg Student Dormitory)
  • Studentenwohnheim Rotenberg (Rotenberg Student Dormitory)

For more details on each dormitory, please refer to:

  • Studentenwohnheim E (Student Dormitory E) (Official Website)
  • Studentenwohnheim Waldhaus (Waldhaus Student Dormitory) (Official Website)
  • Studentenwohnheim Guckelsberg (Guckelsberg Student Dormitory) (Official Website)
  • Studentenwohnheim Rotenberg (Rotenberg Student Dormitory) (Official Website)

Online application address: https://tl1host.eu/SWSB/#home
